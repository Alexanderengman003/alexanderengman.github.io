August, 2018 - June, 2020 | KTH Royal Institute of Technology Stockholm, Sweden

Master of Science

Engineering studies within the field of Nanotechnology, with a nanoelectronics track. The studies were conducted at the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. Areas of study included quantum physics, semiconductor materials and physics, semiconductor devices, nano and microfabrication methods, MEMS and microsystems (RF, microfluidics, CMOS, etc.) The studies were concluded with a Bachelor's thesis titled: "Development and 3D Printing of Intrinsically Stretchable Materials for Supercapacitors ".

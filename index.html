Education

My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in engineering and technology. From bachelor's to master's level, I've delved into cutting-edge fields like nanotechnology and materials science.

August, 2018 - June, 2020 | KTH Royal Institute of Technology Stockholm, Sweden

Master of Science

Engineering studies within the field of Nanotechnology, with a nanoelectronics track. The studies were conducted at the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. Areas of study included quantum physics, semiconductor materials and physics, semiconductor devices, nano and microfabrication methods, MEMS and microsystems (RF, microfluidics, CMOS, etc.) The studies were concluded with a Bachelor's thesis titled: "Development and 3D Printing of Intrinsically Stretchable Materials for Microsupercapacitors ".

August, 2015 - June, 2018 | KTH Royal Institute of Technology Stockholm, Sweden

Bachelor of Science

Engineering studies within the field of Materials Science and Engineering. The studies were conducted at the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. Areas of study included mathematics, physics, chemistry, material properties (ceramics, metals, polymer materials) as well as industrial management and application of novel materials. The studies were concluded with a Bachelor's thesis titled: "Mechanical Properties of Bulk alloys and Cemented Carbides".
